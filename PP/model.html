Business Model

A business model explains how a startup generates revenue and sustains itself financially over time.

Why Business Models Matter

Many startups attract users but fail financially. A business model ensures that value creation translates into sustainable income.

Understanding the Economics

Pricing must reflect customer willingness to pay while covering production and operating costs.

Profit margins determine resilience. Thin margins increase risk during demand fluctuations.

Understanding fixed and variable costs prevents underpricing and unrealistic expectations.
